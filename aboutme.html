My journey

I'm a UI/UX designer, a former Metallurgist and currently residing in Warangal, India. I recently shifted my career into a full time junior frontend developer in the process of rediscovering my creative outlets. I strive to create usable and polished products through passionate and deliberate design.


Through childhood curiosity - of learning how to type and the other using virtual “lego” interfaced code blocks to build a functional code that lights a bulb or moves the toy according to the instructions, had set me gradually on to the path of design.


Though I come from an Engineering background, I'm fascinated in the process of creation, might someday also get a chance to infuse these vast fields of into one to obtaining solutions that attune in the global community in usable and economically viable sorts.
